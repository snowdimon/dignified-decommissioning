## Interdisciplinary Context: Dignified Decommissioning

### Cybernetics & Systems Theory
The problem of the «local maximum trap» and zombie-systems: a system optimized for old conditions continues to consume resources without performing a useful function. Dignified Decommissioning proposes building review triggers and the right to completion into the architecture as a standard mode.

### Philosophy
The ethics of finitude and the «right to die». Unlike cultures obsessed with immortality and endless growth, the principle recognizes that a dignified ending is not a failure, but an act of respect for the future. This echoes Stoicism and *memento mori*.

### Psychology
Fear of loss, the inability to let go (in personal relationships and in projects). Separation from children, the end of a career — Dignified Decommissioning offers a model for a healthy cycle completion with experience extraction, not destruction.
